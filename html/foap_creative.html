how we work

Get obsessed

You get a dedicated team - Account Manager, Creative Director, and Motion Designer who get to know your brand and specific creative needs, review the existing materials and previous campaigns.

Work. Feedback. Work

Together, we work on the briefs for future campaigns. Once the briefs are ready, the team gets to work and delivers first results in 72 hours. We collect your feedback and adjust the assets accordingly.

Ready, steady, ad!

Voila! Now you have the ready-to-be used assets optimized to all the required formats and languages. But that’s not all! We proactively check with you the results of the campaigns and suggest changes to make sure you maximize ROAS.
