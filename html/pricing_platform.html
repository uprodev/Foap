faqs

What is Crowd?

Crowd is a community of content creators. As a brand you can create your own crowd and work with all of the creators who joined it.

What is Mission?

Mission is simply a creative brief, that you can create on our platform for creators to follows. Based on it, they will prepare a content for you to choose from.

Can I choose creators I want to work with?

You can decide and work with all Foap Creators, only your Crowd members or only with

Can I target creators in particular location?

Yes, you can target creators from different countries. Creators from our community comes from 194 countries.

Do I have rights to repurpose creator content internationally?

Yes! Throughout the terms of license, you can repurpose any content from Foap worldwide and across any marketing channel or consumer touch point like TV, email, and print. Furthermore Foap creators adhere to T&Cs that grant rights ownership of the content they produce.

What is your turnaround time for content?

Content turnaround times can vary depending on the complexity of each campaign and its key components including:

  • Campaign goals & KPIs
  • Campaign brief & guidelines
  • Campaign budget
  • Target creator demographics
  • Product fulfillment needs
