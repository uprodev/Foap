faqs

What is Crowd?

Crowd is a community of content creators. As a brand you can create your own crowd and work with all of the creators who joined it.

Do I receive raw footage or ready-to-use ads?

Both.

  • 3 creators deliver raw footage (hooks, pain points and CTA variations)
  • Insense’s post-production team combines them into 18 ready-to-use UGC ads
  • You receive both the ready-to-use ads and all the raw footage

Do I need to pay creators?

No, creator costs are included in the price

Can I choose creators on my own

Yes, you can choose your own creators. Also, we agree our list of creators with you before we start the agreement with the creators.

What makes you different from other agencies?

Compared to an agency, we aren't outsourcing a tool to get the content, instead, we are using our own platform, creator marketplace, and post-production team. This means we know all the best practices to get the most out of the Insense platform and how to optimize creator-brand partnerships for better efficiency.

Do I have the full rights to the video I receive from Foap?

Once you approve a video on Foap, all the licensing rights to it are yours!

What if I don’t like the videos / creators / creative concepts?

2 revisions after delivery are included in the price

How long does it take for your to produce creator video ad?

Yes, our creative team can repurpose your own assets, so they can perform better.
